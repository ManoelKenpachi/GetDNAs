Notes

Crankshaft The crankshaft is steel forged with nitride heat treatment. Nitride heat treatment has the following main customer features: High Strength, Longer Life, Higher Wear Resistance, Higher Seizure Resistance. Conventional practice in crankshaft manufacturing is induction hardening. lsuzu has been a pioneer in nitride heat treatment. All crankshafts used on DURAMAX engines are sent through this process. While nitride heat treatment may cost a little more to implement during the initial manufacturing, the overall life cycle costs have been reduced. Every crankshaft is individually and dynamically balanced to help eliminate vibration. This extra care during the manufacturing process provides for better durability and longer crankshaft life. The crankshaft and flywheel are designed to maintain excellent engine balance, while helping to create the engine's more package-efficient size. The flywheel is not just a simple disc. It has counterweights on the outside that reduce the weight required inside the crankshaft. This more efficient packaging helps to provide additional space for the new charge air cooler located in front of the water radiator. The charge air cooler is used to lower emissions.
